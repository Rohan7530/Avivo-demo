# Company Policies

## Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 18 days of Earned Leave (EL) per year, accrued at 1.5 days per month.
Casual Leave (CL): 6 days per year, non-encashable.
Sick Leave (SL): 7 days per year; a medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.
Maternity/Paternity Leave: 26 weeks for primary caregivers; 2 weeks for secondary caregivers.
Leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance for planned leave,
except in emergencies. Unused EL (up to 30 days) can be carried forward to the next year.

## Work From Home (WFH) Policy

All employees are eligible for up to 2 WFH days per week subject to manager approval.
WFH is not permitted on Mondays or Fridays without explicit VP-level approval.
Employees must be reachable on all official channels (Slack, email, Teams) during WFH.
A stable internet connection (minimum 20 Mbps) is mandatory.
WFH requests must be logged in the HR portal by 9 AM on the day of WFH.
Employees on probation (first 6 months) are not eligible for WFH.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain a respectful and inclusive workplace. Harassment, discrimination,
or bullying of any form will result in immediate disciplinary action up to and including termination.
Confidentiality of company data and client information is mandatory. Employees must not share any
proprietary information on personal devices without VPN and encryption enabled.
Every employee must complete the annual Security Awareness Training by Q1 each year.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately. Side projects that compete with the company's
business are strictly prohibited.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are held bi-annually: in June and December. Ratings are on a 1–5 scale.
Employees scoring below 2 for two consecutive cycles are placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).
Promotions are tied to the December review cycle. Pay revisions are effective from April 1 each year.

## Travel Policy

Domestic travel must be booked at least 5 days in advance via the approved travel portal.
International travel requires department-head approval and 15-day advance notice.
Daily allowance: ₹1,500 for metro cities, ₹1,000 for tier-2 cities. Receipts required for reimbursement.
Economy class for domestic flights; business class permitted only for flights exceeding 6 hours.
