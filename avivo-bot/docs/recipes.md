## 1. Classic Margherita Pizza

**Ingredients:** 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 tsp instant yeast, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp olive oil,
3/4 cup warm water, 1/2 cup tomato sauce, 200g fresh mozzarella, fresh basil leaves.

**Steps:**
1. Mix flour, yeast, salt. Add olive oil and warm water. Knead 8 minutes until smooth.
2. Let dough rise 1 hour covered with a damp cloth in a warm spot.
3. Preheat oven to 250°C. Stretch dough into a 30cm circle on a floured surface.
4. Spread tomato sauce evenly, tear mozzarella over the top.
5. Bake 10–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
6. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil leaves, drizzle with olive oil. Serve immediately.

## 2. Chicken Tikka Masala

**Ingredients:** 500g chicken breast (cubed), 1 cup yogurt, 2 tbsp tikka masala paste,
2 tbsp butter, 1 onion (chopped), 3 garlic cloves, 1-inch ginger, 400g canned tomatoes, 200ml cream.

**Steps:**
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and tikka paste for at least 2 hours (overnight preferred).
2. Grill or pan-fry marinated chicken until charred. Set aside.
3. In a pan, melt butter, sauté onion until golden. Add garlic and ginger, cook 2 minutes.
4. Add canned tomatoes, simmer 15 minutes. Blend sauce until smooth.
5. Return to pan, add cream, stir well. Add cooked chicken, simmer 10 minutes.
6. Garnish with cilantro. Serve with naan or basmati rice.

## 3. Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

**Ingredients:** 2 slices sourdough bread, 1 ripe avocado, 2 eggs, 1 tbsp white vinegar,
salt, pepper, chili flakes, lemon juice, cherry tomatoes.

**Steps:**
1. Toast sourdough bread until golden and crispy.
2. Halve avocado, remove pit, scoop into a bowl. Mash with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer. Add vinegar.
4. Crack each egg into a small cup, swirl the water, and slide egg in. Poach 3 minutes.
5. Spread mashed avocado on toast. Top with poached egg.
6. Sprinkle chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Add halved cherry tomatoes on the side.

## 4. Chocolate Lava Cake

**Ingredients:** 100g dark chocolate (70%), 100g butter, 3 eggs, 3 egg yolks,
80g powdered sugar, 50g all-purpose flour, pinch of salt, vanilla extract.

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a double boiler. Stir until smooth. Cool slightly.
3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until pale and thick (about 3 minutes).
4. Fold chocolate mixture into egg mixture. Add flour and salt, fold gently.
5. Pour batter into ramekins, filling 3/4 full. Bake 10–12 minutes — edges firm, center jiggly.
6. Run a knife around the edge, invert onto plates. Serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.

## 5. Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu

**Ingredients:** 200g firm tofu (cubed), 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil,
1 cup broccoli florets, 1 bell pepper (sliced), 1 carrot (julienned), 2 garlic cloves,
1-inch ginger, 1 tbsp cornstarch, 1/4 cup vegetable broth, sesame seeds.

**Steps:**
1. Press tofu dry with paper towels. Cube and toss with 1 tbsp soy sauce and cornstarch.
2. Pan-fry tofu in sesame oil until golden on all sides. Set aside.
3. In the same pan, sauté garlic and ginger 30 seconds. Add broccoli and carrot, stir-fry 3 minutes.
4. Add bell pepper, stir-fry 2 more minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.
5. Mix remaining soy sauce with vegetable broth, pour over vegetables. Toss to coat.
6. Return tofu to pan, mix gently. Sprinkle sesame seeds. Serve over steamed rice.
