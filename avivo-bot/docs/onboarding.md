# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Aviva Tech!

We're thrilled to have you on board. This guide covers everything you need to do in your first 30 days.
If you have questions, reach out to your buddy, your manager, or People Operations at people@avivatech.com.

## Day 1 Checklist

- [ ] Complete ID verification with Security (Building A, Ground Floor, 9–11 AM slot)
- [ ] Collect your laptop, access card, and equipment from IT (Building B, Room 102)
- [ ] Set up your company email, Slack, and GitHub account (IT will send setup links)
- [ ] Sign the NDA and employment agreement (DocuSign link sent to personal email)
- [ ] Meet your onboarding buddy — they'll show you around and answer questions
- [ ] Schedule a 1:1 with your manager for your first week
- [ ] Complete Security Awareness Training (link in your onboarding email, mandatory Day 1)

## Week 1 Goals

1. **Access Setup:** Ensure you have access to all required systems: Jira, Confluence, GitHub,
   AWS Console (if applicable), Datadog, and the internal admin portal.
2. **Team Introductions:** Join your team's Slack channels. Attend the weekly team standup.
3. **Codebase Walkthrough:** Your buddy will walk you through the monorepo structure.
   Read the Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) in Confluence under "Engineering > ADRs".
4. **Shadow Sessions:** Shadow two colleagues as they work — one from your immediate team,
   one from a cross-functional team (product, design, or data).

## Month 1 Milestones

- Complete all mandatory compliance trainings (GDPR, InfoSec, Diversity & Inclusion)
- Submit your first PR (even if small — documentation fix or test addition counts)
- Present a 5-minute "Hello World" at the monthly all-hands
- Set 3-month OKRs with your manager and submit to HR portal
- Complete the Engineering Onboarding Quiz on Confluence (score ≥ 80% required)

## Benefits Overview

- Health Insurance: Coverage for employee + 2 dependents (policy doc in HR portal)
- Flexible Benefits Allowance: ₹25,000/year for wellness, courses, or home-office equipment
- Learning Budget: ₹50,000/year for conferences, courses, books (requires manager approval)
- Stock Options: Vesting schedule in your offer letter. Questions? Email equity@avivatech.com

## Who to Contact for What

- IT issues: #it-help on Slack or it@avivatech.com
- HR / Leave / Payroll: #hr-help or people@avivatech.com
- Engineering questions: Your team's Slack channel or #engineering
- Security incidents: security@avivatech.com (P1 issues call the on-call number)
- Facilities (office access, parking): facilities@avivatech.com
